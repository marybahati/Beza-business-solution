    About Us

    BEZA TIMES limited is a professional real estate company that deals in project management, property management, real estate consultancy and development.It is a registered company operating in Kenya with offices in Nyanyuki and Timau.We are specialist in offerinf professional management property sercices in both residential and commercial. With manay years of offering good services in the industry, the company is dedicated with competent work force and a large clientele base which includes small and large property investors: both in commercial and residential sector. Over the years we have built internal capacity in key real estate functions critical in ensuring delivery of professional services.

    Our Mission

    Our mission is to consistently provide quality services to our valued clients whilst embracing professional standards and ethics.

    What we offer

    Excellence is our motivation.We offer maintainance, Clearing the premises, Periodic inspection, Letting/Re-letting, Account statements , Rent collection and scheduled periodic meeting on premises performance evaluation.

    Our Vision

    Our corporate vision is to be the market leader and the preferred service provider through partnership that safe guards the interest and goals of all stake holders.
